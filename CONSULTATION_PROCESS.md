**Last Updated**: 2025-06-22  
**Purpose**: Internal reference for managing AI consulting engagements

## Consultation Flow Overview

### 1. Initial Contact & Response (0-24 hours)
- **Contact Sources**: Contact form, direct email, referrals
- **Response Time**: Within 24 hours for business consultations
- **Initial Response**: Acknowledge inquiry, basic availability, next steps

### 2. Consultation Request Processing (24-48 hours)
- **Information Gathering**: Review submitted consultation form details
- **Calendar Coordination**: Propose 2-3 available time slots
- **Meeting Setup**: Send calendar invite with agenda and preparation notes

### 3. Discovery Consultation (30-60 minutes)
- **Format Options**: Phone, video call, or on-site (MS region)
- **Consultation Types**:
  - AI Strategy & Assessment
  - Custom Development Consultation
  - Integration & Implementation Planning
  - Accessibility-First AI Solutions
  - General AI Guidance
  - Strategic Partnerships

### 4. Assessment & Proposal (2-5 business days)
- **Free AI Assessment**: Delivered within 48 hours of meeting
- **Project Proposals**: Custom solutions within 3-5 business days
- **State AI Initiative Inquiries**: Priority response within 24 hours

---

## Consultation Types & Approach

### AI Strategy & Assessment
- **Duration**: 30-45 minutes
- **Focus**: Current processes, AI readiness, opportunity identification
- **Outcome**: Strategic recommendations and implementation roadmap

### Custom AI Development
- **Duration**: 45-60 minutes
- **Focus**: Technical requirements, accessibility considerations, timeline
- **Outcome**: Development proposal with accessibility-first approach

### Integration & Implementation
- **Duration**: 30-45 minutes
- **Focus**: Existing systems, integration challenges, compliance needs
- **Outcome**: Implementation plan with WCAG compliance considerations

### Accessibility-First AI Solutions
- **Duration**: 45-60 minutes
- **Focus**: Accessibility requirements, inclusive design, compliance standards
- **Outcome**: Accessibility-informed AI solution recommendations

---

## The Shepherd Methodology Application

### Discovery Phase
- **Understanding**: Assess current technology landscape and business needs
- **Patience**: Allow time for thorough requirement gathering
- **Guidance**: Provide educational context about AI possibilities and limitations

### Planning Phase
- **Protection**: Ensure solutions include accessibility and security considerations
- **Nurturing**: Develop implementation approach that supports user adoption
- **Practical Focus**: Emphasize measurable outcomes and ROI

### Proposal Phase
- **Accessibility-First**: All solutions include WCAG compliance from the start
- **Proven Frameworks**: Leverage established patterns and methodologies
- **Clear ROI**: Define measurable business outcomes and success metrics

## Post-Consultation Follow-up

### Immediate (Same Day)
- Send thank you note with key discussion points
- Provide any resources or references mentioned
- Confirm next steps and timeline expectations

### Short Term (2-5 days)
- Deliver promised assessments or proposals
- Schedule follow-up meetings if needed
- Provide additional technical resources

### Long Term (Ongoing)
- Maintain relationship for future opportunities
- Share relevant industry insights or updates
- Check in on implementation progress (if engaged)

## Internal Process Notes

### Preparation for Each Consultation:
1. Review contact form submission or initial communication
2. Research company/organization background (if applicable)
3. Prepare relevant case studies or examples
4. Create meeting agenda based on consultation type
5. Set up calendar invite with agenda and preparation notes

### Documentation Requirements:
- Meeting notes with key requirements and discussion points
- Action items and follow-up commitments
- Assessment findings and recommendations
- Proposal development timeline and deliverables

### Follow-up Management:
- CRM entry for new contacts and consultation outcomes
- Calendar scheduling for proposal delivery or follow-up meetings
- Resource sharing and reference materials
- Partnership or collaboration opportunity tracking
