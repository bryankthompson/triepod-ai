### List of Publications

#### 1. Smith, J., et al. (2024). *Evaluation of dietary protein levels for juvenile channel catfish*
- **Research Focus**: Optimal dietary protein levels for juvenile channel catfish
- **Objectives**: Improve growth rates and feed efficiency in aquaculture systems
- **Relevance to AI**: Feeding optimization data could inform AI-driven feeding systems
- **Commercial Application**: Direct impact on feed cost optimization for catfish farmers

#### 2. Thompson, A., & Lee, B. (2025). *Genetic selection for disease resistance in catfish: A review*
- **Research Focus**: Genetic selection strategies for disease resistance enhancement
- **Objectives**: Sustainable aquaculture practices through improved genetics
- **Relevance to AI**: Genetic data integration with AI monitoring systems for breeding programs
- **Commercial Application**: Reduced disease-related losses through better breeding decisions

#### 3. Green, C., & Brown, D. (2023). *Nutritional strategies to enhance immune function in catfish*
- **Research Focus**: Dietary approaches to boost immune function
- **Objectives**: Health and disease prevention in aquaculture systems
- **Relevance to AI**: Nutrition monitoring integration with AI health assessment systems
- **Commercial Application**: Preventive health strategies reducing medication costs

#### 4. White, E., et al. (2024). *Breeding programs for improved growth rates in channel catfish*
- **Research Focus**: Selective breeding programs for growth rate enhancement
- **Objectives**: Higher productivity in farming operations
- **Relevance to AI**: Growth prediction models and breeding decision support systems
- **Commercial Application**: Faster time-to-market and improved farm profitability

#### 5. Black, F., & Smith, G. (2025). *Application of genomics in catfish aquaculture*
- **Research Focus**: Genomic techniques for trait improvement
- **Objectives**: Enhanced growth and disease resistance through precision breeding
- **Relevance to AI**: Genomic data integration with AI prediction models
- **Commercial Application**: Precision aquaculture and optimized breeding programs
