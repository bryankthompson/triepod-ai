## Official Government Announcement

### Primary Source: Governor Tate Reeves Official Website

**Date**: June 18, 2025  
**Title**: "State of Mississippi to advance artificial intelligence education, innovation and workforce development with NVIDIA"

**Partnership Structure**
- **Type**: Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) between Mississippi and NVIDIA
- **Primary Focus**: AI education expansion, research collaboration, workforce development, economic development
- **Educational Partner**: Mississippi Artificial Intelligence Network (MAIN)

**Key Industries Targeted**
1. **Agriculture** - Explicitly mentioned as priority sector

3. **Energy** - Grid optimization and efficiency
4. **Defense** - Cybersecurity and AI applications
