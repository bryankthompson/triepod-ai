# Mississippi Catfish Industry Publications Research

**Research Date**: January 21, 2025  
**Purpose**: Identify key industry publications for content curation and commentary strategy

---

## Key Industry Publications for Mississippi Catfish Sector

### 1. The Catfish Journal
- **Publishing Organization**: Catfish Farmers of America (CFA)
- **Website**: https://www.catfishfarmersofamerica.com/
- **Content Type**: Official trade publication published six times annually
- **Coverage**: Current industry information, market trends, farming practices, regulatory issues
- **Target Audience**: U.S. catfish farmers, industry stakeholders
- **Relevance to AI/Technology**: Primary trade publication for U.S. catfish farmers - ideal venue for commentary on AI adoption and technology innovations. Members receive biweekly news alerts and industry updates.

### 2. Mississippi State University Extension Service - Catfish Publications
- **Publishing Organization**: Mississippi State University Extension Service
- **Website**: http://extension.msstate.edu/agriculture/catfish
- **Key Contacts**: Dr. Jimmy Avery and MSU Extension specialists
- **Content Type**: Technical bulletins, research reports, newsletters, best practices guides
- **Coverage**: Production systems, water quality, nutrition, disease management, marketing, economics
- **Relevance to AI/Technology**: **HIGH** - MSU is actively researching precision agriculture and technology adoption in aquaculture. Their extension publications would be authoritative sources for discussing AI applications in catfish farming.

### 3. USDA National Agricultural Statistics Service (NASS) - Catfish Production Reports
- **Publishing Organization**: U.S. Department of Agriculture
- **Website**: https://www.nass.usda.gov/Surveys/Guide_to_NASS_Surveys/Catfish_Production/
- **Content Type**: Monthly and annual statistical reports, production data, economic indicators
- **Coverage**: Production volumes, inventory levels, sales, prices, regional statistics (Mississippi is the largest producer)
- **Publication Schedule**: Monthly releases with annual summaries
- **Relevance to AI/Technology**: Provides baseline data essential for AI research and can document productivity improvements from technology adoption. Recent reports available through January 2025.

### 4. World Aquaculture Magazine
- **Publishing Organization**: World Aquaculture Society (WAS)
- **Website**: https://www.was.org/Magazine/About
- **Content Type**: Quarterly magazine with peer-reviewed articles, industry news, technical features
- **Coverage**: Global aquaculture trends, research developments, technology innovations, sustainability practices
- **Relevance to AI/Technology**: **HIGH** - Covers emerging technologies in aquaculture globally, making it an excellent venue for discussing AI applications in catfish farming within the broader aquaculture context.

### 5. Aquaculture Magazine
- **Publishing Organization**: International aquaculture industry publication
- **Website**: https://aquaculturemag.com/
- **Content Type**: Industry magazine covering global aquaculture developments, technical articles, market analysis
- **Coverage**: Production techniques, disease management, feed technology, sustainability, market trends
- **Target Audience**: Global aquaculture industry professionals
- **Relevance to AI/Technology**: **EXCELLENT** - Platform for technical articles on AI and automation in aquaculture. Covers innovation and technology adoption across all aquaculture species including catfish.
