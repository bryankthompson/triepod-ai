## Content Guidelines Reminders

### Always Include:
- ✅ Results and outcomes (85% success rate, $16K value)
- ✅ Time savings (48-hour turnaround)
- ✅ Cost reduction (95% savings)
- ✅ Real-world validation
- ✅ Professional benefits

### Never Reveal:
- ❌ Specific memory system technologies (Neo4j, Chroma, Qdrant)
- ❌ Detailed implementation code
- ❌ Proprietary algorithms
- ❌ Client-specific information
- ❌ Internal scoring mechanisms

### Tone & Style:
- Professional but accessible
- Educational without being condescending
- Confident about results
- Mysterious about methods
- Empowering for users
